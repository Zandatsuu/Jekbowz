Oracle Redbull Racing

Red Bull Racing also known as Red Bull or RBR competing as Oracle Red Bull Racing
is a Formula One team competing under an Austrian license and based in the United Kingdom
The Red Bull Racing team is one of two teams owned by the energy drink company Red Bull GmbH the other being AlphaTauri
This team has been led by Christian Horner since its formation in the 2005 season until now.
